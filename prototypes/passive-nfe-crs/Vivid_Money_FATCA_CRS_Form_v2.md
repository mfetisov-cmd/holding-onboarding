## APPENDIX — DEFINITIONS

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Account Holder** | The entity that holds the financial account with Vivid Money SA. For CRS purposes, this is the person or entity listed or identified as the holder of a Financial Account by the Financial Institution that maintains the account. |
| **U.S. Person** | Under FATCA, a U.S. person includes: (a) a U.S. citizen, (b) a U.S. resident alien, (c) a domestic partnership, (d) a domestic corporation, (e) any estate other than a foreign estate, or (f) any trust if a court within the United States exercises primary supervision over its administration and one or more U.S. persons have authority to control all substantial decisions of the trust. |
| **TIN (Taxpayer Identification Number)** | A unique number assigned by a jurisdiction to identify a taxpayer. Examples include: Germany — Steuernummer or Steuer-ID; France — SIREN/SIRET; Netherlands — RSIN; Luxembourg — Matricule/RCS number; Spain — NIF/CIF. |
| **Financial Institution** | An entity that is a Custodial Institution, a Depository Institution, an Investment Entity, or a Specified Insurance Company as defined under CRS and FATCA. |
| **Investment Entity** | An entity that primarily conducts as a business one or more of: (a) trading in money market instruments, foreign exchange, transferable securities, or commodity futures; (b) individual and collective portfolio management; or (c) investing, administering, or managing financial assets or money on behalf of other persons. |
| **NFE / NFFE (Non-Financial Entity)** | Any entity that is not a Financial Institution. Under CRS referred to as NFE (Non-Financial Entity); under FATCA referred to as NFFE (Non-Financial Foreign Entity). |
| **Active NFE** | An NFE where: (a) ≥50% of the entity's gross income for the preceding calendar year is from active business operations (not passive income such as dividends, interest, rents, royalties); OR (b) ≥50% of the entity's assets during the preceding calendar year were assets that produced or were held for the production of active income. Also includes publicly traded companies, government entities, international organizations, central banks, and certain holding companies of non-financial groups. |
| **Passive NFE** | An NFE that is not an Active NFE. Typically entities whose income is primarily from dividends, interest, rents, royalties, annuities, or gains from the sale of assets that produce such income. Examples: dormant holding companies, IP holding entities, SPVs, family offices, passive investment vehicles. |
| **Controlling Person** | The natural person(s) who exercise control over an entity. For companies, this generally means: (a) any individual who directly or indirectly holds ≥25% of the ownership interests (shares, voting rights); or (b) if no individual meets the 25% threshold, the natural person(s) who exercise control through other means (e.g., senior managing officials). For trusts: the settlor(s), trustee(s), protector(s), beneficiaries, and any other natural person exercising ultimate effective control. |
| **Controlling Person Type** | The nature of the control: "Ownership" = direct/indirect equity holding ≥25%; "Control by other means" = senior managing official, or control exercised through arrangements, relationships, or other means not based on equity ownership. |
| **Reportable Account** | A Financial Account maintained by a Reporting Financial Institution and held by one or more Reportable Persons, or by a Passive NFE with one or more Controlling Persons that is a Reportable Person. |
| **Reportable Person** | A person who is tax resident in a Reportable Jurisdiction (under CRS) or a Specified U.S. Person (under FATCA). Excludes: listed corporations, government entities, international organizations, central banks, and financial institutions. |
| **U.S. Reportable Account** | Under FATCA, a Financial Account held by one or more Specified U.S. Persons or by a non-U.S. entity with one or more Controlling Persons that is a Specified U.S. Person. |
